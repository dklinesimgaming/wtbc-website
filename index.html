Welcome to the Club

Willenhall Trinity Bowls Club has been a cornerstone of the local community since 1921 — over a century of crown green bowls in the heart of Willenhall. We are a friendly, welcoming club that takes pride in being open to all ages and abilities.

Whether you're a seasoned competitor or have never picked up a wood in your life, you'll find a warm welcome here. We play every day of the week, so there's always a game on and a friendly face to greet you.

Membership is by arrangement — just get in touch and we'll be happy to talk you through how to join. We'd love to see you on the green!
