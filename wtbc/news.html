Junior Taster Sessions Starting April

We are delighted to announce that we'll be running free taster sessions for juniors aged 10–17 this April. No experience necessary — all equipment will be provided and our friendly coaches will be on hand to help.

Sessions will run every Saturday morning in April from 10:00am to 12:00pm. Places are limited so please contact us to register your interest.
